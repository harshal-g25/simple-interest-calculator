# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be kind and respectful
- Accept constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders will enforce standards and take corrective action.

## Scope
Applies to all community spaces and representation of the community.

## Enforcement
Abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to leaders.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Consequences may include: warning, temporary ban, permanent ban.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.0.
